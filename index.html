The Learn Enough Story

Learn Enough to Be Dangerous is a leader in the movement to teach technical sophistication, the seemingly magical ability to take command of your computer and get it tto do your bidding. This includes everything from command lines and coding to guessing keyboard shortcuts, Googling error messages, and knowing when to just reboot the darn thing.
